Feeding

Feeding tab gives you the ability to track on which side the last feeding was done to the baby. This is more useful for the exclusive breast feeding moms.

Pumping / Stash

The Pumping or Stash tabs allows exclusive pumping moms the ability to track how much milk is being pumped and where it is stored.

App Usage

The base version of the app allows you to log 50 entries (combined of feeding, pumping and stash).

This limit can be extended by paying for the "Full Access" version through the app menu.

Milk Expiry

Based on CDC and several other trusted sources, we calculate an approximate best before date for the milk that is stored in Fridge, Freezer and Deep Freezer.
This is just for indication, we request you to follow your doctor's advice on the right expiry limits for the milk since this totally depends on various factors.

Timer

The app has a built in timer feature that allows you to log the start time and duration of pumping or feeding in an easier way.

About Dairy Store

A simple and clean interface for Exclusive Breast Feeding or Exclusive Pumping moms to log their daily data.

This pump log app allows you to track your Breastfeed or Breast pump data so that you get an overview of your production. You will be able to understand on which days of the week your production is more.
